Oureum: Your Stable Digital Asset

Oureum is a fully asset-backed, transparent, and redeemable stablecoin. It provides the stability of traditional currency with the benefits of blockchain technology, making it ideal for a variety of financial applications.

OUM: The Scalable and Secure Network

OUM is an Ethereum Layer 2 network with zero gas fees. It offers high scalability with over 1,000 TPS, ensuring fast and efficient transactions. OUM provides Ethereum-grade security and is EVM-compatible, making it developer-friendly.
